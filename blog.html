Durgapuja Second Post

Durgapuja is one of the biggest Bengali festval celebrated in the month of september-october during autumn season.It is celebrated for 9 days,where "Saptami","Astami","Navami" and "Dashami" play a very important role.

Goddess Durga is worshiped during this festival.The festival celebrates the victory of Goddess Durga(Woman Power) over "Mahisasura"(Devil).The Puja begins from Mahalaya.

The pandals are very beautifully designed for this ocation.The pandals mostly furnished by two types of design-One is in Traditional way and another is theme based.The idols of Goddess Durga with her children "Saraswati","laxmi","karthik","Ganesh" are kept in pandals which are worshiped by the devotees.The Festival ends with the immersion of the idol of Goddess Durga in water bodies on Vijaya Dashami
